About Us

Shubh Aarambh Events Inc. was officially established as a registered entity in February 2022. Its primary objective is to provide entertainment and foster a sense of joy within the community by organizing vibrant Garba/Navratri celebrations accompanied by delectable food, uninterrupted dancing, and soul-stirring music.

The inception of Shubh Aarambh Events Inc. stemmed from a general desire to engage and entertain our community during the challenging period of the Covid lockdown. As a group of enthusiastic young friends, we felt a need to connect with our community and contribute positively to their well-being. Given our Gujarati background, the idea of organizing a Garba event naturally resonated with us, allowing us to bring together Garba enthusiasts to revel in the festivities of Navratri and pay homage to the goddess Durga.

From that point forward, our journey has been filled with continuous growth and inspiration. We have been fortunate to encounter the right opportunities at the right time, enabling us to streamline our event planning processes effectively. Collaborating with dedicated community members and volunteers, we tirelessly strive to promote local arts, culture, and heritage through an array of programs and events. These initiatives encompass Navratri events, musical nights, festivals, dramas, and even film promotions, both on a local scale and across international platforms.

Our commitment to organizing memorable events and fostering community engagement remains unwavering. Through the medium of Shubh Aarambh Events Inc., we endeavor to create enriching experiences that celebrate our cultural heritage while uniting individuals in moments of joy, entertainment, and celebration.
